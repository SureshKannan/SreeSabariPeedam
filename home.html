Welcome to Sri Sabari Peedam

Sri Sabari Peedam is a registered, non-profit religious institution which was established in 2010 to assist the Brampton’s Hindu community with worshiping needs. The temple was visioned and founded by Arun Swamy who sought to build a temple that accurately followed the proper Agama.
The Maha Kumbhabhishekam (inauguration) was performed in July 2010. The deities at the main temple include Kanni Mula Maha Ganapathy, Maalikaipurathu Amman, Periya Kadutha, Sriya Kadutha Sawamy, Sani Pagavaan and Manikandar (in puli vaganam) were installed according the agamas and yantras.
